About the Practice

Where Art Meets Dental Science

Founded by Dr. Elena Vasquez, Luminance Smile Design has become the go-to destination for patients seeking truly artistic cosmetic dentistry in South Jersey. Every treatment is designed with meticulous attention to facial proportion, skin tone, and individual personality.

Dr. Vasquez trained at NYU College of Dentistry and completed advanced aesthetic training at the Las Vegas Institute. Her work has been featured in regional publications and she continues to teach cosmetic techniques to fellow dentists.
