The Periodontist

Dr. James Novak, DMD, MS

Board-certified periodontist with 18 years of focused experience in gum disease treatment, implant surgery, and tissue regeneration. Dr. Novak completed his periodontal residency at Rutgers School of Dental Medicine and is a Diplomate of the American Board of Periodontology.

He is one of few periodontists in South Jersey certified in LANAP laser therapy, offering patients a minimally invasive alternative to traditional gum surgery. His practice philosophy centers on saving teeth whenever possible and providing the most conservative effective treatment.
