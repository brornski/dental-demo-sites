The Surgeon

Dr. Marcus Chen, DDS, FICOI

Board-certified oral surgeon and Fellow of the International Congress of Oral Implantologists, Dr. Chen has placed over 3,200 implants with a 98% long-term success rate. He completed advanced surgical training at Columbia University and specializes in complex full-arch rehabilitation and guided implant surgery.

His philosophy is simple: every patient deserves a predictable outcome, minimal discomfort, and a result that lasts decades. The Precision Implant Center in Voorhees was built around this principle, featuring an in-office CBCT scanner, digital surgical planning suite, and a dedicated surgical operatory.
