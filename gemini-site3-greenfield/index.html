About Our Practice

Where Families Come First

Dr. Rachel Greenfield founded this practice over 15 years ago with a simple belief: dental care should be comfortable, transparent, and accessible for every member of the family. A graduate of the University of Pennsylvania School of Dental Medicine, Dr. Greenfield has built a reputation for patience, precision, and genuinely personal care.

Our Cherry Hill office is designed with families in mind. A dedicated children's play area, comfort amenities for anxious patients, and state-of-the-art digital imaging ensure that every visit is as smooth and stress-free as possible.
