The Riverstone Difference

Not Your Typical Dental Office

Every detail has been designed to make you feel calm, comfortable, and cared for from the moment you arrive.

Sensory Comfort Suite

Each treatment room features dimmable lighting, aromatherapy diffusers, heated neck pillows, and paraffin hand wax treatments. You set the mood -- we handle the rest.

  • Lavender, eucalyptus, and citrus aromatherapy
  • Weighted blankets available on request
  • Noise-canceling headphones with your music

Sedation Options for Every Level

Whether you need mild relaxation or full sedation, our board-certified team tailors the approach to your comfort level. No judgment, no pressure -- just gentle care.

  • Nitrous oxide (laughing gas)
  • Oral conscious sedation
  • IV sedation for extensive procedures

Holistic Wellness Approach

We believe oral health and whole-body wellness are connected. Our team takes time to understand your health history and creates treatment plans that support your overall well-being.

  • Mercury-free, biocompatible materials
  • Oral-systemic health assessments
  • Minimally invasive treatment philosophy
