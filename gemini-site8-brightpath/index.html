What We Do

Dental Care Made Fun

Specialized pediatric services from your child's first tooth through their teenage years.

Kid-Friendly Exams

Gentle checkups with ceiling TVs, fun sunglasses, and a treasure chest after every visit. We make exams something to look forward to.

Sealants & Fluoride

Preventive treatments that create an invisible shield against cavities. Quick, painless, and highly effective for cavity-prone teeth.

Early Orthodontics

Age 7 evaluations to catch bite and alignment issues early. Early intervention can prevent longer, more complex treatment later.

Gentle Sedation

Safe, comfortable sedation options for anxious little ones. Our team has specialized training in pediatric sedation techniques.

Special Needs Care

Experienced, patient care for children with developmental, physical, or sensory challenges. Every child deserves a comfortable dental home.

Emergency Care

Knocked-out tooth? Toothache at bedtime? We offer same-day emergency appointments because dental emergencies do not wait.

Meet the Team

Dr. Sarah Kim, DMD

Board-certified pediatric dentist and mom of two. Dr. Kim completed her pediatric residency at the Children's Hospital of Philadelphia and has been creating happy smiles in South Jersey for over 14 years.

She founded BrightPath with a simple idea: the dentist should be a place kids actually want to go. With adventure-themed treatment rooms, a play area in the lobby, and a team trained to work with even the most anxious children, we have turned that vision into reality.

Your Child's First Visit

We have designed every step to put kids (and parents) at ease.

1

Meet & Greet

Your child picks a themed room and meets their dental buddy for the day.

2

3

Treasure Chest
