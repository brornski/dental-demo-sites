About the Dentist

Dr. Natalie Rivers, DDS

After years of watching patients avoid needed dental care out of fear, Dr. Rivers decided to build a practice entirely around comfort. She founded Riverstone Dental Spa to prove that exceptional dentistry and deep relaxation are not mutually exclusive.

A graduate of Temple University School of Dentistry with advanced training in sedation and cosmetic dentistry, Dr. Rivers has transformed thousands of anxious patients into regular visitors who genuinely look forward to their appointments.
