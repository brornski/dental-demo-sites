About the Orthodontist

Dr. Priya Sharma, DMD, MS

Dr. Sharma is a board-certified orthodontist and Invisalign Diamond+ Provider, placing her in the top 1% of Invisalign practitioners nationally. After completing her orthodontic residency at the University of Pennsylvania, she launched Align Orthodontics with a mission to bring technology-driven, patient-centered care to South Jersey.

Her practice uses iTero Element 5D scanning, AI-powered treatment simulation, and remote monitoring to deliver faster results with fewer appointments. Every treatment plan is customized to your unique dental anatomy, lifestyle, and goals.
