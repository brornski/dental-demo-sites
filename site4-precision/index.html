Our Procedures

Implant Solutions for Every Need

From single tooth replacement to full-arch restoration, we offer the full spectrum of implant procedures.

Single Tooth Implants

Individual titanium implant with a custom ceramic crown. Looks, feels, and functions like your natural tooth. Typical healing time: 3-4 months.

All-on-4 Full Arch

Complete upper or lower arch restoration using just four strategically placed implants. Walk out with a full set of teeth in a single day.

Bone Grafting

Advanced bone regeneration techniques using synthetic and donor materials to build the foundation for successful implant placement.

3D-Guided Surgery

Computer-planned, digitally guided implant placement for pinpoint accuracy, minimal invasion, and predictable outcomes every time.

Meet Your Surgeon

Dr. Kim is a dual-degree oral and maxillofacial surgeon with over 20 years of experience in implant dentistry. He completed his surgical residency at the Hospital of the University of Pennsylvania and holds board certification from the American Board of Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery.

He has placed over 5,000 dental implants and is recognized as one of the top implant surgeons in the greater Philadelphia area. Patients from across the tri-state area seek his expertise for complex cases.
