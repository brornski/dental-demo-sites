Periodontal Health

Conditions We Treat

Gum disease affects nearly half of adults over 30. Left untreated, it can lead to tooth loss and systemic health problems. We specialize in identifying, treating, and preventing periodontal disease at every stage.

Request a Gum Health Evaluation

Gingivitis

Early-stage gum inflammation with bleeding and swelling. Highly treatable when caught early.

Periodontitis

Advanced gum disease causing bone loss and pocket formation around teeth.

Gum Recession

Exposed tooth roots from receding gums, causing sensitivity and risk of decay.

Peri-Implantitis

Inflammation and bone loss around dental implants requiring specialized treatment.

Our Treatments

Advanced Periodontal Procedures

We use the latest technology and techniques to treat gum disease with precision and minimal discomfort.

LANAP Laser Therapy

FDA-cleared laser protocol that targets diseased gum tissue while preserving healthy tissue. No scalpels, no sutures, and significantly faster healing compared to traditional surgery.

No Cutting Fast Recovery FDA-Cleared

Gum Grafting and Regeneration

Soft tissue grafts and guided tissue regeneration to rebuild lost gum and bone. We use growth factors and advanced biologics to promote natural healing and tissue regrowth.

Tissue Rebuild Biologics Growth Factors

Scaling and Root Planing

Deep cleaning below the gumline to remove tartar, bacteria, and toxins. Smooths root surfaces to help gums reattach and prevent further disease progression.

Deep Clean Non-Surgical Preventive
